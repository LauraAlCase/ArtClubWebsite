About Art Club

Here in art club we aim to cultivate interest in art throught our LHS community. Although we part-take in more intriquet art form such as classical-Renissance peinture we welcome people of all skillsets. Be prepared for fun activities such as pumpkin carving and snowflake cutting. We also part-take in the culinary arts such as baking and cupcake decorating. Seasonal foods are promised! Art Club is where it's at! It's a place of dreams,fun, and community service hours. Improving the Community through art.
